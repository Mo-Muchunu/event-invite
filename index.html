Upcycling Workshop

What’s it all about?

Join our community-powered upcycling workshop where creativity meets sustainability! It's a space for everyone to come together, share ideas, and learn new skills. Breathe new life into unwanted items – from clothes and furniture to old bikes! Whether you’re a seasoned DIYer or a complete beginner, come along and revive an item - save it from landfill!

  • Date: Saturday, 20th July, 2025
  • Time: 11 AM – 4 PM
  • Entry: Adults - £6 Children - FREE
    (All proceeds donatedto local food bank.)
  • Venue: Open Hub, 1 side Lane, Ware
  • Directions: Map

What Can You Upcycle?

Bring something from home you’d like to work on or pick from our selection of pre-loved items. Our expert team of crafty helpers will be on-site to help. There will be workstations set up for different projects, so you can choose what you want to work on. Whether it’s sewing, painting, or woodworking, we’ve got you covered!
We’ll provide the tools, materials, and inspiration – you just need to bring your creativity and enthusiasm!

Refuel & Refresh

We’ll have a selection of delicious homemade cakes, pastries and refreshments available for purchase including tea, coffee, and soft drinks to keep you fueled and energised all day.
Vegan and kid-friendly options provided too. Feel free to bring your own lunch if you prefer!

Transform your old items into something new and beautiful!
